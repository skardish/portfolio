Isometric Illustration

The idea behind this project is to illustrate a process in isometric proportion.

Overview

The process of my Isometric Illustration is the construction of a hamburger. Construction workers are hard at work collecting all the different toppings needed to create the perfect burger. They get the ingrediants from the fields, to the construction site, and off to the stores.
