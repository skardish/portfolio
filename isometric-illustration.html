Isometric Illustration

The idea behind this project is to illustrate a process in isometric proportion. This piece demonstrates my ability to create a digital illustration in a 3 dimensional space.

Overview

The theme of my Isometric Illustration is the construction of a hamburger. The idea was to take the theme literally and have construction workers collecting everything you would need for a hamburger. Starting from a farm, to the construction process itself, and off to a restaurant.
