Lockitron

App Design

This project was a collaberative project. The task at hand was to create an app to solve a problem for Algonquin College residence. After several interviews it was decided it would be best to create a smart application that gives users the ability to control certain features in their residence room with their smart phone.

Sketchs

Features

There are several features this app consists of. Including, a feature to lock and unlock your door, temperature control, light control, and scheduling.

Final Design

Overall Lockitron is targeted at students living in residence. The interface itself is very simple and easy to use, great for the fast paced lifestyle of a student.
